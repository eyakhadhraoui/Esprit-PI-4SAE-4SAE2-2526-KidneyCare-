Comprehensive Kidney Care for Children

We provide specialized diagnosis and treatment for pediatric kidney diseases including urinary tract infections, nephrotic syndrome, congenital kidney disorders, and chronic kidney disease. Our goal is to ensure healthy growth and kidney function for every child through personalized and preventive care.

Experienced Pediatric Nephrology Team

Our qualified pediatric nephrologists specialize in managing complex kidney conditions in infants, children, and adolescents. We ensure continuous monitoring, laboratory follow-up, and family-centered care to improve long-term health outcomes.

Dialysis and Emergency Kidney Support

We provide advanced pediatric dialysis services and emergency care for acute kidney injury and severe renal complications. Our unit ensures safe hospitalization, daily monitoring, and specialized medical support tailored for children.
